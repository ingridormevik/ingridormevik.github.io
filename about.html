About

I'm a Digital Culture BA student at the University of Bergen, currently interning with the SurvivingSOGICE research project at the Centre for Digital Narrative.

My work sits at the intersection of digital ethics, queer activism, and critical AI studies. I explore how algorithmic systems shape identity, visibility, and community safety—both through academic research and hands-on digital activism.

I serve as Digital Media Strategist for Bergen Pride and board member of FRI Vestland, Norway's organization for gender and sexuality diversity, where I develop content strategies that navigate platform governance and algorithmic suppression.

Current Work

  • Research intern: SurvivingSOGICE project (January–May 2026)
  • BA thesis research on digital ethics and platform governance (2026-2027)
  • Digital activism combating algorithmic bias in social media

Research Interests

  • AI ethics and algorithmic bias
  • Platform governance and content moderation
  • Digital activism and queer visibility
  • Surveillance capitalism and privacy
  • Interactive fiction and digital narratives
